- **Corpus:** 3M+ annotations, 386K egocentric clips, game/cinematic footage — licensable or white-labelable
- **Workforce:** 2,000+ collectors, 100+ cities, 5+ countries — can run their annotation pipelines
- **Platform:** Annotation infra built for video/robotics CV

What makes a strong supply partner:
- They collect video we don't have (specific environments, regions, tasks)
- They have distribution to AI labs we're not reaching
- They need annotation capacity or corpus inventory to scale
