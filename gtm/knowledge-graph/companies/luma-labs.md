## Overview
Luma AI (lumalabs.ai) is a generative AI company building world models and video/image generation tools. Their flagship product Dream Machine produces professional-grade video and images; their Ray3 model is the first "reasoning video model" capable of physically accurate simulation. In late 2025 they raised $900M Series C at a $4B valuation, with infrastructure targeted at training large-scale world models.

## Data Gap / Relationship
Luma's world model ambitions require massive amounts of diverse real-world video — egocentric, physical interactions, environmental context. They need high-quality labeled video datasets to train models that understand physical reality. Claru's egocentric and physical AI datasets are directly relevant.

## Pitch Angle
Luma's world models need video that reflects physical reality — not stock footage or synthetic data. Claru can supply curated egocentric video, human motion, and environmental datasets captured across 100+ cities, with annotation layers that teach models how the physical world actually works. Reach out post-Ray3 launch when their data appetite will be at its peak.
