## Overview
DYNA Robotics builds production-ready robotic foundation models for manufacturing automation. Their DYNA-1 model achieved 99%+ success rate over 24+ hours of non-stop operation. Founded by Lindon Gao + York Yang (sold Caper AI for $350M) and Jason Ma (ex-DeepMind). Raised $120M Series A (Sept 2025) from NVIDIA, Amazon, Salesforce, Samsung, LG at $600M+ valuation. Prior $23.5M seed (March 2025). Based on similar thesis to Physical Intelligence.

## Data Gap / Relationship
DYNA's foundation model approach is exactly like Physical Intelligence — they need diverse human demonstration data to train general manipulation capabilities. The DYNA-1 model needs to extend beyond napkin folding to new manufacturing tasks, each requiring new training data. Claru's human activity video and manipulation datasets are directly applicable. NVIDIA and Amazon backing means they have budget.

## Pitch Angle
DYNA just raised $120M from NVIDIA and Amazon to build production-ready manipulation models. Their DYNA-1 proves the approach works for repetitive tasks — the next phase is generalizing to new factory tasks, each requiring demonstration data. Claru can supply human demonstration video of the specific manufacturing tasks DYNA is targeting, with annotation schemas designed for foundation model training (action labeling, state annotations, success/failure labels). Reach through Lindon Gao or their data/ML team.
