Intenseye builds AI-powered workplace health and safety monitoring using computer vision. Analyzes camera feeds 24/7 for safety violations (PPE compliance, near-misses, ergonomic risks). CEO Sercan Esen. Raised $90M+ total ($64M Series B led by Lightspeed, Feb 2024). Deployed at global enterprises (pharma, manufacturing, logistics). The largest-ever investment in AI-powered workplace safety.

## Data Gap / Relationship
Intenseye's models need labeled video of workers performing tasks across diverse industrial environments — correct vs. incorrect PPE usage, ergonomic risks, near-miss events, etc. Each new industry or environment type (pharmaceutical cleanroom, construction site, warehouse) requires new training data. Claru can commission workplace video capture and annotation across specific industry settings.

## Pitch Angle
Intenseye's models generalize to new industries when they have labeled video from those environments. As they expand from manufacturing to new sectors (healthcare, construction, food processing), Claru can supply annotated video of workers in those environments performing the specific activities Intenseye's models need to detect. Sercan Esen or their ML team are the contacts.
