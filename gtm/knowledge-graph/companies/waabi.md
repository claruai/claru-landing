## Overview
Waabi is a Toronto-based AV company building generalist autonomy AI. Founded by Raquel Urtasun (ex-Uber ATG chief scientist). Raised $1B Series C (Jan 2026) co-led by Khosla + G2 Venture Partners + Uber, for 25,000 robotaxi deployment on Uber's platform. Total $1.28B raised. CNBC Disruptor 50 (2025). Their CoPilot 4D model and AI simulator are their core tech.
