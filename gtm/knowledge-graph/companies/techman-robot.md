## Overview
Techman Robot is a Taiwanese cobot maker (subsidiary of Quanta Computer) expanding into humanoid robots. Their TM Xplore I humanoid (unveiled 2025, targeting H2 2026 launch) is a wheeled humanoid for industrial and service applications using NVIDIA Jetson Orin. Notably, at GTC 2026 they showcased a humanoid training system using j-mex motion capture — indicating active need for human motion demonstration data. Revenue: $56.8M in 2025 (23% YoY growth).
