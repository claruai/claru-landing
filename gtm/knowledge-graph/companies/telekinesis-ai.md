## Overview
Telekinesis is a European physical AI platform (Germany) enabling factory floor operators to deploy robots without coding, using LLM and VLM-based planning. Backed by EIC (European Innovation Council), Techstars, and German VCs. CEO Suman Pal. Raised $2.08M. Spin-off from top European robotics labs. Targets SMBs for small-batch manufacturing with 12-month ROI claim. Early stage.
