## Overview
General Robotics (generalrobotics.company) is an early-stage US startup building general-purpose AI intelligence for robots — any robot, any form, any real-world scenario. Their GRID platform modularly composes AI skills for robots to sense, reason, and act. Selected for Microsoft for Startups Pegasus Program (Nov 2025). Partnered with Trossen Robotics. Very early stage; funding not publicly disclosed.
