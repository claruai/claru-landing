## Overview

Figure AI is one of the best-funded humanoid robotics companies in the world ($1.9B raised, $39B valuation as of Series C in Sept 2025). Founded and led by Brett Adcock, they build Figure 02 and Figure 03 humanoid robots for industrial deployment. They ended their OpenAI partnership in February 2025 to build AI entirely in-house. They are deployed at BMW factories. Brett Adcock opened Claru's Wave 4 email. They are a Tier 1 target for egocentric training data.

## Hiring Signals

Figure is aggressively hiring across robotics, ML, and manufacturing. They run BotQ, a dedicated manufacturing facility targeting 100K robots over 4 years. Data collection and annotation roles likely exist internally but volume needs far exceed internal capacity.

## What They've Actually Shipped

- **Figure 02**: First commercial humanoid deployed at BMW manufacturing facility. Performing real industrial tasks.
- **Figure 03**: Launched October 2025. Next-gen humanoid with improved dexterity and speed.
- **BotQ**: Dedicated manufacturing facility in San Jose, operational March 2025. Capacity: 12,000 humanoids/year, targeting 100,000 over 4 years.
- **In-house AI**: After ending OpenAI partnership in Feb 2025, Figure builds its own multimodal robotics AI. Their models need massive amounts of robot demonstration and egocentric training data.
- **Revenue**: First paying customer (BMW) with contract value undisclosed but implied to be significant given Series C pricing.

## Data Gap / Relationship

Figure's in-house AI development (post-OpenAI) means they need their own training data pipeline. To train Figure 03 (and future robots) to perform diverse household and industrial tasks, they need: (1) human demonstration videos from first-person (egocentric) perspective showing manipulation tasks, (2) diverse environment video showing the environments Figure robots will work in (homes, factories, warehouses), (3) annotation of object interactions, grasp types, force application, etc. This is exactly Claru's core service. Figure is also an Avala client target — moving quickly is essential.
