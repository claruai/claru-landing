## Overview
Nexobot is an early-stage Melbourne-based startup building modular AMR parcel-sorting robots for small-to-mid-sized logistics companies on a RaaS model. Very early stage — raised A$400K pre-seed (Skalata + Antler). Won inaugural Propel-AIR MassRobotics accelerator in Aug 2025. CEO Dominic Lindsay.
