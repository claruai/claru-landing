## Overview
Runway is a NYC-based AI video generation company and one of the top-3 video gen models (with Sora and Luma). Founded 2018 by Cristóbal Valenzuela, Alejandro Matamala, Anastasis Germanidis. Raised $315M Series E (Feb 2026) at $5.3B valuation, led by General Atlantic. Partners with NVIDIA Rubin platform. Gen 4.5 model is #1 on Video Arena leaderboard, emphasizing physics, human motion, camera movement. World model ambitions.
