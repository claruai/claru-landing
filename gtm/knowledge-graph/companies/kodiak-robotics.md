## Overview
Kodiak AI (formerly Kodiak Robotics) went public on Nasdaq Sept 25, 2025 via SPAC merger with Ares Acquisition Corp II, valued at ~$2.5B. CEO Don Burnette (founder). First company to deliver driverless semi-trucks to a paying customer (Atlas Energy Solutions, 100-truck order). Secured $275M+ through the public transaction. Their Kodiak Driver is a full-stack AV system for Class 8 trucks.

## Data Gap / Relationship
Kodiak's AI needs driving scenario data for diverse highway and off-road environments — weather conditions, construction zones, mining environments. As they expand off-road, they need video data of off-highway environments. Secondary: their pedestrian detection models need human activity data in industrial/logistics environments.
