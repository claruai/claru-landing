## Overview

Galaxea AI is a Beijing-based humanoid robotics company founded in September 2023 by scientists from Tsinghua and Stanford. They build industrial/productivity humanoid robots — G0 (Aug 2025), G0 Plus (Jan 2026). Their R1 Pro robot supports Ant Group's open-source LingBot-VLA model. Rapidly funded: raised $291M in April 2026 (total ~$435M+, valuation 20B+ yuan). One of China's top-tier humanoid robotics unicorns.

## Data Gap / Relationship

Same pattern as other VLA-based humanoid companies: Galaxea needs diverse human demonstration video to train their LingBot-VLA implementation, especially for generalization across industrial environments outside China. The Western data angle is Claru's differentiator — Galaxea can get Chinese factory data internally but would need NA/EU environment footage for export markets. However, China-based company with China focus creates geopolitical and data-sovereignty considerations for partnership feasibility. Lower priority than US-based humanoid companies.
