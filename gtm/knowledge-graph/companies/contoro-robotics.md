## Overview
Contoro Robotics builds AI-powered robots for autonomous unloading of floor-loaded shipping containers and trailers. Uses a human-in-the-loop (HITL) model ensuring >99% success rate. Austin TX. Raised $22M total including $13.5M Series A (March 2025) from Amazon Industrial Innovation Fund, Doosan, Coupang, and others. CEO Youngmok Yun (ex-Harmonic Bionics).
