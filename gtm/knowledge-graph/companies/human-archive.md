## Overview
Human Archive is a YC W26 startup (founded 2025) building a multimodal data collection and annotation platform for robotics and world modeling. They deploy custom hardware in residential and manufacturing environments to capture real-world egocentric and dexterous manipulation data, then QA, anonymize, and annotate it for frontier robotics labs. Their stated goal is to build "the Common Crawl for human sensorimotor intelligence." They sent an inbound inquiry to Claru on Apr 2.
