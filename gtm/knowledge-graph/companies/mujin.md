## Overview
Mujin is a Tokyo-based industrial automation company building MujinOS — an intelligent robotics platform combining real-time digital twin, motion planning, and orchestration for manufacturing and logistics. Founded 2011, raised $233M Series D (Dec 2025) led by NTT Group and Qatar Investment Authority, total $411M. CEO Ross Diankov (co-founder). Expanding globally with NTT and Salesforce backing.
