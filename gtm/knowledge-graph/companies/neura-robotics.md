## Overview
NEURA Robotics is a German humanoid and cognitive robotics company building robots that work alongside humans in manufacturing, logistics, and healthcare. Founded 2019, they raised €120M Series B (Jan 2025) and were reportedly raising €1B more (Mar 2026) backed by Tether at a €4B valuation. CEO David Reger has a €1B+ order book. Their 4NE-1 Mini humanoid understands natural language and is designed for human-robot collaboration environments.
