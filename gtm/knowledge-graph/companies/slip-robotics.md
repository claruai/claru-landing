## Overview
Slip Robotics builds autonomous loading/unloading robots (SlipBots) for truck docks that can load/unload any trailer in 5 minutes with zero IT integration. Founded 2019, Atlanta GA. Raised $28M Series B (Dec 2024) led by DCVC, total $45M. CEO Christopher Smith. Customers include John Deere, GE Appliances, Valeo, Nissan.
