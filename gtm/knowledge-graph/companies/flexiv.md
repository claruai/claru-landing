## Overview
Flexiv is a general-purpose robotics company integrating force control, computer vision, and AI for adaptive robots used in assembly automation, surface treatment, and product testing. Founded 2016, HQ in Silicon Valley + China offices. Raised $322M total including a $100M Series C in June 2025. Their Rizon and Moonlight robots are deployed in manufacturing environments requiring high precision and adaptability.

## Data Gap / Relationship
Force-controlled assembly robots need training data from the specific assembly tasks they perform — with force feedback labels, trajectory demonstrations, and failure case examples. Claru can supply human demonstration video of assembly and manipulation tasks with fine-grained annotation. Also valuable: egocentric video of skilled assembly workers performing tasks that Flexiv's robots are meant to replicate.

## Pitch Angle
Flexiv's Rizon robot needs to generalize across assembly variations — new parts, tolerances, sequences. Human demonstration data of skilled assemblers annotated with task decomposition is the training substrate for this. Claru can commission factory floor capture and annotation across manufacturing environments that match Flexiv's target deployments. Shiquan Wang or their AI/data team are the contacts.
