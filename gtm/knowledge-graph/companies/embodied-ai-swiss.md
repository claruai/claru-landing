## Overview
Embodied AI (formerly Helix Robotics) is a Lausanne Swiss startup (EPFL spin-off) building VLA (Vision-Language-Action) models for soft robotic manipulators. CEO Francesco Stella (PhD EPFL, ex-MIT). Their Helix Manipulator is a soft robotic arm with 9 DOF, 1m reach, 1cm accuracy. Funded by Venture Kick (CHF 150K). Very early stage. Patents published in Nature npj Robotics.
