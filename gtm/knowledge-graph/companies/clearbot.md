## Overview
Clearbot builds autonomous electric boats for ocean and waterway waste cleanup. Founded by University of Hong Kong graduates Sidhant Gupta and Utkarsh Goel. 13 boats deployed across India and Hong Kong. Backed by Razer. Very early stage — niche application. Limited Claru relevance as their AI is primarily for marine waste detection (floating debris recognition), not human activity data.
