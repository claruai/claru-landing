## Overview

Alegion is an Austin-based enterprise data labeling company founded in 2012, specializing in complex video and image annotation with an integrated ML-assisted platform. They have served Fortune 500 companies and government agencies across defense imagery, agritech drone footage, autonomous vehicles, and industrial safety. Their strength is the annotation platform and managed services -- they do not collect raw data, making them a natural partner for a company that does.

## What They've Actually Shipped

- **Alegion Control**: Self-serve annotation platform, initially video-focused, with ML-assisted labeling that reduces annotation time by up to 80%
- **Enterprise video annotation**: Frame-by-frame object tracking across video sequences for autonomous vehicles, security surveillance, sports analysis, construction safety
- **Government/defense work**: Defense-related imagery analysis (specific contracts not disclosed)
- **Agritech**: Crop and livestock labeling in drone footage
- **Azure + AWS Marketplace listings**: Enterprise procurement-ready
- **Global workforce partnerships**: StepWise (Certified B Corp in Kenya), Impact Centers in Egypt and Uganda

## Data Gap (why they'd need Claru)

1. **Zero collection capability.** Alegion is purely annotation infrastructure. They label data others bring to them. No cameras, no collectors, no field operations.
2. **Platform, not people-in-the-field.** Their value prop is the annotation software and managed workforce -- but as clients demand end-to-end (collect + annotate + deliver), Alegion can't serve the full pipeline alone.
3. **Video annotation strength is stranded without video supply.** They claim industry-leading streaming video annotation, but frontier robotics labs need someone to capture the video first.
4. **Government/enterprise clients shifting to physical AI.** Defense and autonomous vehicle clients are increasingly requesting egocentric and embodied AI data -- Alegion's existing clients may be asking for data types they can't source.
5. **Small team, lean model.** 51 employees means they need partners, not headcount growth, to expand into new modalities.

## Pitch Angle (subcontractor)

> "Alegion has the strongest enterprise video annotation platform in the market, but your clients are increasingly asking for physical AI data that doesn't exist yet -- egocentric video from real environments, multi-sensor captures, teleoperation recordings. We're the collection side of that equation. 2,000+ trained collectors in 100+ US cities capturing exactly what your annotation platform needs to label. We deliver raw, structured video; your platform and workforce annotate it. Your enterprise and government clients get end-to-end without either of us building what the other already has."
