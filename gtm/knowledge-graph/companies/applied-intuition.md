## Overview

Applied Intuition is a Mountain View-based autonomy software platform company — they build simulation, tools, and software infrastructure for self-driving cars, industrial machines, and defense autonomous systems. Founded by Qasar Younis (ex-YC COO) and Peter Ludwig. Valued at $15B after $600M Series F in June 2025 (co-led by BlackRock + Kleiner Perkins). Serves 18 of 20 largest global OEMs. Now expanding into defense (took US Army vehicle from bare-bones to autonomous in 10 days).

## Data Gap / Relationship

Applied Intuition is primarily a software/simulation company, not a data consumer. They build the simulation tools that other companies use to generate synthetic training data. However, they do work with real-world validation data to calibrate simulations. Claru's angle: real-world AV/outdoor environment video could be used by Applied Intuition to validate simulation accuracy ("sim-to-real gap" calibration). But this is a stretch — Applied Intuition likely has better-funded data partnerships already. The ICP score is lower because they're a platform company, not a data-hungry robot company.
