## Overview
Origami Robotics (YC) builds a high-DOF robotic hand with in-joint motors and a co-designed data-collection glove that matches the hand exactly — eliminating the embodiment gap. Their key insight: the glove generates human demonstration data that transfers directly to robot hardware without sim-to-real gap. Focused on general manipulation. Very early stage.
