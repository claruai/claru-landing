## Overview
LookDeep Health is an Oakland-based AI company building computer vision and agentic AI for hospital care. Their platform uses cameras and CV to continuously monitor patients for fall prevention and safety events; their latest product "aimee" is a hospital-ready agentic AI that observes the real world in real time alongside nurses and doctors. They self-describe their approach as "physical AI with multimodal models" — notable framing for a healthcare company.

## Data Gap / Relationship
LookDeep's aimee platform requires training on diverse hospital video scenarios — patient fall events, fall-risk behaviors, clinical interactions, bedside activity. They need labeled video data of human physical behavior in healthcare environments. Claru's human activity and egocentric video datasets, plus annotation expertise, could accelerate their model training.
