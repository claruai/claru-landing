## Overview

Aescape is a New York-based robotics company building AI-powered robotic massage systems. Founded in 2017 by Eric Litman, they raised $83M in March 2025 (led by Valor Equity Partners) and have deployed robotic massage tables across 100+ locations including Equinox gyms. Their AI system scans a person's body in real time and adapts the massage to their anatomy, pressure preferences, and tension spots. Eric Litman appears to have left the company — Frank Britt is now the correct CEO contact per Claru's pipeline. Tom Brady joined as an investor/ambassador in Sept 2025.

## Data Gap / Relationship

Aescape's AI needs: (1) body posture/anatomy video for their 3D body scan model, (2) egocentric or third-person video of massage techniques for training massage-quality classifiers, (3) diverse human body type video for generalization across demographics. This is physical AI training data squarely in Claru's wheelhouse. However, the specific modality is body-scan data — they may need specialized medical/wellness camera setups that differ from standard egocentric rigs. The more likely angle is: Claru collects diverse human-body-position and movement video (using existing egocentric collection infrastructure) that trains their body recognition model to handle different body types, environments, and positioning scenarios.
