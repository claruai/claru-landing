## Overview
WeRide is a Chinese AV company (Guangzhou-founded, now global) building robotaxis and autonomous vehicles. Founded 2017 by Tony Han (ex-Baidu chief scientist). Dual-listed on Nasdaq (Oct 2024) and HKEX (Nov 2025) at $5.3B valuation. Raised $1.09B total. Uber, Grab, and Bosch are anchor investors in HKEX IPO.
