## Overview

Decart is an Israeli AI lab building real-time interactive AI video models. Their flagship product Oasis is a real-time world model that transforms live footage into immersive digital environments — essentially a real-time game engine driven by AI. They hit $3.1B valuation in Aug 2025 after their $100M Series B. Metar Megiora (data team) replied to Claru's Wave 4 outreach saying it sounds interesting and asking for pricing and catalog details — making this an active warm lead.

## What They've Actually Shipped

- **Oasis**: Real-time world model that reached 1M+ users within 3 days of release. Transforms live footage into interactive digital environments with sub-second latency.
- **MirageLSD**: Enhanced real-time video transformation with higher quality and lower latency.
- **GPU optimization stack**: Multi-million dollar contracts with cloud providers and AI labs, reducing cost per hour of AI-generated content from $10-$1000s to under $0.25.
- Revenue from day one via B2B GPU optimization licensing.

## Data Gap / Relationship

Decart trains world models on video data — they need large-scale, diverse, high-quality video to train Oasis and future models. Their data team (Metar Megiora) reached out after Wave 4, specifically asking about pricing and amounts. The fit is primarily on the video data side — they need video depicting real-world physics, human interaction, and environmental diversity. Claru's egocentric corpus and game environment datasets are potentially relevant. The robotics/physical-AI angle is less direct than with humanoid companies, but world models need exactly the kind of naturalistic video Claru collects.

## Pitch Angle

Lead with the video data catalog — egocentric POV footage, game environment captures, activity-dense clips. Emphasize volume, diversity of environments, and annotation depth. Position Claru as the source for grounded, physically-realistic video that world models need (vs. scraped internet video which is uncontrolled and potentially copyright-encumbered). Send the data catalog link + pricing sheet directly to Metar. Don't pitch robotics specifically — pitch world model training data.
