## Overview
One Robot (YC) builds world models for robot evaluation and training. Their simulations are "realistic to see and realistic to interact with" — enabling robotics teams to train and evaluate VLA (vision-language-action) models without being bottlenecked by physical robot time. Founded 2025 by Hemanth Sarabu and Elton Shon. Very early stage (2 employees).

## Data Gap / Relationship
One Robot's world models need real-world video data to train realistic simulation physics and appearance. Claru's diverse real-world video (egocentric, manipulation, environments) is exactly what their world models need as training data. This is a high-alignment partnership opportunity.

## Pitch Angle
One Robot's world models are only as good as the real-world data they're trained on. Claru can supply diverse real-world video of human manipulation, household environments, and physical interactions to improve the realism of their simulation. Even 2-person team YC companies listen to smart pitches. Hemanth Sarabu or Elton Shon are the contacts.
