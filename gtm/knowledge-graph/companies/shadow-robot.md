## Overview
Shadow Robot Company is a London-based robotics company founded 1987, specializing in dexterous robotic hands with 24 movements and 20 degrees of freedom. Their DEX-EE and DEXterous Hands are used for manipulation research globally and in hazardous environments. Only ~20 employees — very lean. Received ARIA funding for advancing robot dexterity (May 2025). Key customer: researchers and labs building dexterous manipulation AI.
