## Overview
ANYbotics is a Zurich-based robotics company building four-legged autonomous robots for industrial inspection in hazardous environments (oil & gas, mining, power, metals). Their ANYmal robots perform thousands of weekly inspections across 200+ deployed units. Raised €127M+ in Sept 2025, bringing total to $150M+. CEO Dr. Péter Fankhauser. ANYmal X (Ex-certified for explosive zones) targeted for 2026 customer delivery.
