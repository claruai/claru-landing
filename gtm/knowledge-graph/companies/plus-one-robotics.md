## Overview
Plus One Robotics builds AI-powered vision systems and parcel-handling robots for logistics and e-commerce. Their system combines computer vision, AI, and supervised autonomy — robots handle routine parcel picking, and human operators remotely assist for edge cases. Raised $100M total (Series C $50M, March 2023, led by Scale Venture Partners). CEO Erik Nieves. San Antonio, TX.

## Data Gap / Relationship
Their supervised autonomy model generates data of human corrections for edge cases — which is exactly the kind of imitation learning data that improves their AI. Claru can augment this by supplying diverse parcel handling video across edge case scenarios: unusual packaging, damaged goods, unfamiliar barcodes, non-standard shapes.

## Pitch Angle
Plus One's supervised autonomy model works because humans correct AI mistakes — those corrections are training data. Claru can accelerate this loop by commissioning intentional edge-case video of unusual parcel types, challenging orientations, and hard-to-identify items. Erik Nieves or their ML team are the right contacts.
