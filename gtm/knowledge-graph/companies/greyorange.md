## Overview
GreyOrange is a US-headquartered (Suwanee, GA) warehouse automation company building AI-powered AMRs and the Ranger platform for fulfillment operations. Raised $545M total including $135M Series D. CEO Akash Gupta. Partnered with Google Cloud on GreyMatter DeepNav — an AI tool to deploy AMRs 80% faster in new warehouses. Active leadership expansion in 2025.
