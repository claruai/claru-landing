## Overview
Snorkel AI builds programmatic data labeling — using LLMs and weak supervision to label data with minimal human annotation. $100M Series D (May 2025) at $1.3B valuation. CEO Alex Ratner (ex-Stanford AI Lab). $148M revenue (2025). Their approach reduces human annotation need — somewhat against Claru's model. But for physical/video data where LLM labeling falls short, Claru's human collection is complementary.
