## Overview
Monumental (Amsterdam) builds autonomous ground vehicles for on-site construction automation, starting with bricklaying. Founded by Salar al Khafaji (ex-Palantir/Silk) and Sebastiaan Visser. Raised $25M Series B (Feb 2024). Deployed on European construction sites (Netherlands, partnerships with top 25 European contractors). Addresses EU labor shortage in construction.

## Data Gap / Relationship
Construction robots need training data of humans doing construction work — carrying materials, laying blocks, navigating rough terrain, interacting with scaffolding. As Monumental expands task types, they need diverse video of construction workers performing those tasks. Claru can commission construction site video capture and annotation across European cities.

## Pitch Angle
