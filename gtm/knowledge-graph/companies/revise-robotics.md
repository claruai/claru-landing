## Overview
Revise Robotics automates consumer electronics refurbishment (smartphones, laptops, etc.) using robotics and computer vision. Founded 2024 by Rupesh Jeyaram and Antonio Monreal. Very early — 3 employees. YC backed. Their robots need to recognize diverse electronics models, identify damage, and perform disassembly/reassembly tasks.
