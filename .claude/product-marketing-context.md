## What We Do
Claru builds and delivers purpose-built training datasets for frontier robotics, embodied AI, and world model companies. We capture real-world video (not synthetic, not scraped), enrich it with depth maps, pose estimation, segmentation masks, and structured metadata, then have expert humans annotate intent, context, and edge cases. We deliver training-ready data in your format, on your timeline.

## Ideal Customer Profile
- Frontier AI labs building world models (video generation, physical simulation)
- Robotics companies training VLAs (Vision-Language-Action models)
- Embodied AI researchers needing egocentric/first-person video datasets
- Companies training autonomous systems (driving, manipulation, navigation)
- AI labs needing RLHF/preference data for video or multimodal models

## Key Differentiators vs. Competitors
1. **Real-world, licensed data** — Not synthetic, not scraped. Every clip comes from our network of 10,000+ trained collectors across 100+ cities.
2. **Enriched by default** — Every clip ships with depth maps, pose estimation, segmentation masks, optical flow, and action labels. Competitors sell raw video; we sell training-ready data.
3. **Expert human annotation** — Our annotators label what machines miss: intent, context, edge cases, grasp types, object affordances.
4. **Scale + Speed** — 3.7M+ completed annotations. 25+ active datasets. Brief to first delivery in days, not months.
5. **Physical AI focus** — We're not a generic data labeling shop. We specialize in the data modalities that physical AI needs: egocentric video, teleoperation recordings, manipulation sequences, multi-view captures.

## Competitive Landscape
- **Luel** (YC W26) — Two-sided marketplace for rights-cleared multimodal data. ~60 blog posts, strong SEO. Weakness: no deep enrichment pipeline, launched 6 weeks ago.
- **Scale AI** — Enterprise data labeling at scale. Weakness: generalist, not specialized for physical AI. Expensive, slow.
- **Appen** — Legacy crowd-sourced data labeling. Weakness: quality issues, not specialized for video/robotics.
- **Labellerr** — Annotation platform. Weakness: tools not data — they don't capture or deliver datasets.
