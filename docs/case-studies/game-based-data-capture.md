## Summary
Built a custom video game capture solution that records high-fidelity gameplay footage alongside timestamped raw keystroke and control data. No off-the-shelf tool existed for this requirement. Delivered 10,000+ hours of synchronized gameplay and control data across first-person and third-person 3D games, enabling simulation of human behavior in complex 3D environments.

## Key Metrics
- 10,000+ hours of synchronized data
- Custom capture solution built from scratch
- Raw timestamped control inputs + video

## What We Did
Designed a custom application for simultaneous screen recording and raw input logging. Every keystroke was captured as structured data with precise timestamps, enabling frame-level alignment between video and control inputs.

The solution scaled across different games and player profiles, and supported sustained long-running capture sessions without data loss or drift.

## Why It Matters
High-quality simulation requires understanding how humans actually interact with environments — not just what they see, but what they do. Without synchronized control data, systems must infer intent from visuals alone. This closes a critical gap between observation and behavior, unlocking new possibilities for training agents in complex 3D worlds.
