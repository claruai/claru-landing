## Summary
Executed a multi-phase benchmark to identify the most effective prompt enhancement solution for text and video generation. 180 prompts were evaluated across both modalities. Text evaluation used side-by-side comparisons with 3 annotators. Video evaluation used pairwise comparisons with multiple annotators. Two models emerged as clear statistical leaders, delivering a confident production recommendation.

## Key Metrics
- 180 prompts across 2 modalities
- Clear winner identification for video workflows
- Reusable evaluation framework established

## What We Did
The text phase used side-by-side comparisons with 3 independent annotators selecting the best enhanced prompt. The video phase used pairwise comparisons focused on downstream output quality — evaluating the enhanced prompts by what they actually produced.

Both phases assessed holistic quality across intent preservation, structural clarity, and effectiveness. Results were aggregated across annotators to surface consistent patterns and eliminate individual bias.

## Why It Matters
Prompt enhancement directly shapes output quality, cost, and trust — especially for video generation where iteration cycles are expensive. This engagement replaced guesswork with large-scale human evaluation, establishing a governed and repeatable framework for a decision that impacts every downstream output.
